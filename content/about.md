COMPANY OVERVIEW

## About AWJ Innovation

AWJ Innovation is a brand name of the Sustainable Technologies & Innovation Development Company (STID), an endowment and philanthropic entity. We identify opportunities and fill gaps in innovation within the Sultanate of Oman, creating programs to strengthen young Omani talents, foster new businesses, and drive disruptive technologies for job and wealth creation.

OUR STORY

## Pioneering Excellence Since 2020

### The Beginning

Founded in 2020 with a vision to transform Oman's innovation landscape through locally-driven solutions that bridge global technology and regional needs.

### Growth & Expansion

Evolved into four specialized units - Innovation, Academy, Sustain, and Systems - each addressing specific development needs with excellence.

### Today & Beyond

From Knowledge Oasis Muscat, we lead transformation through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships for sustainable impact.
