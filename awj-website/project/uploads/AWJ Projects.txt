First 3D Printed Building in the Middle East
AWJ Innovation partnered with German University of Technology in Oman (GUtech) to create the first 3D printed building in the Middle East. This groundbreaking project represents a significant milestone in construction technology and sustainable building practices.

The project demonstrated the potential for advanced manufacturing technologies in construction, reducing material waste by 60% and construction time by 70%. This innovation has paved the way for sustainable construction practices across the region and positioned Oman as a leader in construction technology.
