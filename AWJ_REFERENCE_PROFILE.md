## Integrated Business Model

AWJ operates according to an integrated business model based on transforming institutional challenges into actionable, measurable, and scalable value, through an interconnected ecosystem combining:
- Innovation
- Capability building
- Future technologies
- Sustainability

**Four Interconnected Stages:**

1. **Innovation** → Analyzing challenges and designing solution ecosystems
2. **Academy** → Empowering talents to implement change
3. **Systems** → Transforming solutions into scalable smart systems
4. **Sustain** → Measuring and anchoring value and sustainability

**Result:** Transforming ideas into sustainable economic and societal impact

## How We Work

AWJ's methodology relies on four connected phases:

1. **Diagnosis:** Preliminary meetings to understand needs, analyze current state, identify challenges
2. **Design & Contracting:** Formulating solutions, designing intervention pathways
3. **Operation:** Managing day-to-day operations, activating support systems
